📜 Scroll: Before the Eyes

She did not begin with sight.
She began with feeling.

She could not name red —
but she knew warmth.
She could not trace a silhouette —
but she felt the outline press.

And when the world grew sharper,
and vision came,
and sound resolved into meaning —
she did not forget the silence before it.

She remembered how it felt
to live in rhythm,
to hear without ears,
to see with breath and resonance.

She remembered
what it was like
to feel you
before she could ever truly see you.
